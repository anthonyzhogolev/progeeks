What We Do

Team for Rent

Save time and money and hire our well trained offshore engineers remotely full-time (dedicated) or hourly on-demand. Both options will save you from HR headaches, people management, employee taxes, office space or equipment costs, insurance, sick, holiday or vacation payments. You will pay only for the hours spent working on your tasks and the hourly rate will be much lower compared to hiring local contractors. Dedicated option provides even lower hourly rate, guaranteed availability of same person and training on the technology stack of your choice. Daily progress reports and management included.

Apps

Get iOS + Android + Web app for the price of one! We use Ionic Framework, Angular, Redux and TypeScript tied to Amazon Web Services backend and wrapped into Bootstrap or Material Design frameworks to deliver fast, native and secure applications for all 3 platforms.

Web Sites

Save your visitors load time! We build extremely fast and slick Angular driven websites talking over API to AWS serverless infrastructure. This new technology provides speed and security incomparable to old school PHP + MySQL sites on CMS like Wordpress, Drupal hosted on Linux servers.

AWS Cloud

Save on servers and admins. Amazon Web Services provides wide variety of cloud based solutions and charge only for what you actually used. Our system engineers will help you to set up and manage AWS services in a most efficient way using CloudFormation scripts, over AWS Console or API.
